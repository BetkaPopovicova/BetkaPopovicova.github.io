1. řádný termín

$\left(\frac{1}{4} + \frac{5}{6}\right) \cdot \left(\frac{5}{13} - \frac{1}{2}\right)$


$ =\left(\frac{1\,\cdot\,3\,+\,5\,\cdot\,2}{12}\right) \cdot \left(\frac{5\,\cdot\,2-\,1\,\cdot\,13}{26}\right) =\left(\frac{3\,+\,10}{12}\right) \cdot \left(\frac{10\,-\,13}{26}\right) $ $ =\frac{13}{12} \cdot \frac{-3}{26} =\frac{\cancel{\color{blue}13}}{\cancel{\color{green}12}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{green}-3}}{\cancel{\color{blue}26}} $ $ =\frac{1}{4} \cdot \frac{-1}{2} =-\frac{1}{8} $

$$\frac{\frac{6}{5}}{\frac{7}{6} \cdot 4 - 4 \cdot \frac{5}{12}}$$


$$ =\frac{\frac{6}{5}}{\frac{7}{6} \cdot \frac{4}{1} - \frac{4}{1} \cdot \frac{5}{12}} =\frac{\frac{6}{5}}{\frac{7}{\cancel{\color{red}6}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{red}4}}{1} - \frac{\cancel{\color{blue}4}}{1} \cdot \frac{5}{\cancel{\color{blue}12}}} =\frac{\frac{6}{5}}{\frac{7}{3} \cdot \frac{2}{1} - \frac{1}{1} \cdot \frac{5}{3}} $$ $$ =\frac{\frac{6}{5}}{\frac{14}{3} - \frac{5}{3}} =\frac{\frac{6}{5}}{\frac{9}{3}} =\frac{\frac{6}{5}}{\frac{\cancel{\color{red}9}}{\cancel{\color{red}3}}} =\frac{\frac{6}{5}}{\frac{3}{1}} $$ $ =\frac{6}{5} \cdot \frac{1}{3} =\frac{\cancel{\color{green}6}}{5} \cdot \frac{1}{\cancel{\color{green}3}} =\frac{2}{5} \cdot \frac{1}{1} =\frac{2}{5} $
