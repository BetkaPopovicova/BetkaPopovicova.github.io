1. řádný termín

$\frac{5}{9} - \frac{5}{9} : 5$


$ =\frac{5}{9} - \frac{5}{9} \cdot \frac{1}{5} =\frac{5}{9} - \frac{\cancel{\color{red}5}}{9} \cdot \frac{1}{\cancel{\color{red}5}} $ $ =\frac{5}{9} - \frac{1}{9} =\frac{4}{9} $

$\frac{4 - 7}{8} \cdot \frac{16}{21}$


$ =\frac{-3}{8} \cdot \frac{16}{21} = = \frac{\cancel{\color{red}-3}}{\cancel{\color{green}8}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{green}16}}{\cancel{\color{red}21}} $ $ =\frac{-1}{1} \cdot \frac{2}{7} =-\frac{2}{7} $

$$\frac{\frac{3}{5} : \left(\frac{2}{5} + \frac{1}{2}\right)}{\frac{7}{6} + \frac{7}{10}}$$


$$ =\frac{\frac{3}{5} : \left(\frac{2\,\cdot\,2\,+\,1\,\cdot\,5}{10}\right)}{\frac{7\,\cdot\,5\,+\,7\,\cdot\,3}{30}} =\frac{\frac{3}{5} : \left(\frac{4\,+\,5}{10}\right)}{\frac{35\,+\,21}{30}} =\frac{\frac{3}{5} : \frac{9}{10}}{\frac{56}{30}} $$ $$ =\frac{\frac{\cancel{\color{green}3}}{\cancel{\color{blue}5}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{blue}10}}{\cancel{\color{green}9}}}{\frac{\cancel{\color{red}56}}{\cancel{\color{red}30}}} =\frac{\frac{1}{1} \cdot \frac{2}{3}}{\frac{28}{15}} $$ $ =\frac{2}{3} \cdot \frac{15}{28} =\frac{\cancel{\color{red}2}}{\cancel{\color{green}3}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{green}15}}{\cancel{\color{red}28}} =\frac{1}{1} \cdot \frac{5}{14} =\frac{5}{14} $

2. řádný termín

$\frac{1}{3} \cdot \frac{1}{2} - \frac{8}{9}$


$ =\frac{1}{6} - \frac{8}{9} =\frac{1\,\cdot\,3\,-\,8\,\cdot\,2}{18} =\frac{3\,-\,16}{18} =\frac{-13}{18} =-\frac{13}{18} $

$\left(2 - \frac{5}{6}\right) : \frac{5}{3}$


$ =\left(\frac{2\,\cdot\,6\,-\,5}{6}\right) : \frac{5}{3} =\frac{12\,-\,5}{6} : \frac{5}{3} =\frac{7}{\cancel{\color{red}6}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{red}3}}{5} =\frac{7}{2} \cdot \frac{1}{5} =\frac{7}{10} $

$$\frac{\frac{2}{3} + \frac{2}{7}}{\left(\frac{9}{14} + \frac{3}{2}\right) \cdot 2}$$


$$ =\frac{\frac{2\,\cdot\,7\,+\,2\,\cdot\,3}{21}}{\left(\frac{9\,+\,3\,\cdot\,7}{14}\right) \cdot 2} =\frac{\frac{14\,+\,6}{21}}{\left(\frac{9\,+\,21}{14}\right) \cdot 2} =\frac{\frac{20}{21}}{\frac{\cancel{\color{red}30}}{\cancel{\color{red}14}}\cdot 2} =\frac{\frac{20}{21}}{\frac{15}{7} \cdot \frac{2}{1}} $$ $ =\frac{20}{21} \cdot \frac{7}{30} =\frac{\cancel{\color{red}20}}{\cancel{\color{green}21}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{green}7}}{\cancel{\color{red}30}} =\frac{2}{3} \cdot \frac{1}{3} =\frac{2}{9} $

1. náhradní termín

$3 \cdot \frac{2}{7} - \frac{2}{7}$


$ =\frac{3}{1} \cdot \frac{2}{7} - \frac{2}{7} =\frac{6}{7} - \frac{2}{7} =\frac{4}{7} $

$1 - \frac{14}{5} : 2$


$ =1 - \frac{14}{5} : \frac{2}{1} =1 - \frac{\cancel{\color{red}14}}{5} \cdot \frac{1}{\cancel{\color{red}2}} =1 - \frac{7}{5} \cdot \frac{1}{1} $ $ =1 - \frac{7}{5} =\frac{1\,\cdot\,5\,-\,7}{5} =\frac{5\,-\,7}{5} =\frac{-2}{5} =-\frac{2}{5} $

$$\frac{\frac{3}{4} + \frac{4}{3}}{\frac{5}{7} \cdot \frac{14}{3}}$$


$$ =\frac{\frac{3\,\cdot\,3\,+\,4\,\cdot\,4}{12}}{\frac{5}{\cancel{\color{red}7}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{red}14}}{3}} =\frac{\frac{9\,+\,16}{12}}{\frac{5}{1}\cdot\frac{2}{3}} =\frac{\frac{25}{12}}{\frac{10}{3}} $$ $ =\frac{25}{12} \cdot \frac{3}{10} =\frac{\cancel{\color{red}25}}{\cancel{\color{green}12}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{green}3}}{\cancel{\color{red}10}} =\frac{5}{4} \cdot \frac{1}{2} =\frac{5}{8} $

2. náhradní termín

$$\frac{\frac{2}{3} - 1}{\frac{8}{9}}$$


$$ =\frac{\frac{2\,-\,3}{3}}{\frac{8}{9}} =\frac{\frac{-1}{3}}{\frac{8}{9}} $$ $ =\frac{-1}{3} \cdot \frac{9}{8} =\frac{-1}{\cancel{\color{red}3}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{red}9}}{8} =\frac{-1}{1} \cdot \frac{3}{8} =\frac{-3}{8} =-\frac{3}{8} $

$2 \cdot \frac{1}{6} - \frac{3}{8} \cdot 4$


$ = \frac{2}{1} \cdot \frac{1}{6} - \frac{3}{8} \cdot \frac{4}{1} = \frac{\cancel{\color{red}2}}{1} \cdot \frac{1}{\cancel{\color{red}6}} - \frac{3}{\cancel{\color{green}8}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{green}4}}{1} $ $ =\frac{1}{1} \cdot \frac{1}{3} - \frac{3}{2} \cdot \frac{1}{1} =\frac{1}{3} - \frac{3}{2} =\frac{1\,\cdot\,2\,-\,3\,\cdot\,3}{6} $ $ =\frac{2\,-\,9}{6} =\frac{-7}{6} =-\frac{7}{6} $

$$\frac{\frac{6}{7} - \frac{9}{14}}{\frac{8}{7} + \frac{6}{7} : \frac{3}{2}}$$


$$ =\frac{\frac{6\,\cdot\,2\,-\,9}{14}}{\frac{8}{7} + \frac{\cancel{\color{red}6}}{7} \cdot \frac{2}{\cancel{\color{red}3}}} =\frac{\frac{12\,-\,9}{14}}{\frac{8}{7} + \frac{2}{7} \cdot \frac{2}{1}} =\frac{\frac{3}{14}}{\frac{8}{7} + \frac{4}{7}} =\frac{\frac{3}{14}}{\frac{12}{7}} $$ $ =\frac{3}{14} \cdot \frac{7}{12} =\frac{\cancel{\color{red}3}}{\cancel{\color{green}14}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{green}7}}{\cancel{\color{red}12}} =\frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{1}{4} =\frac{1}{8} $
