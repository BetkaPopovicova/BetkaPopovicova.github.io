1. řádný termín

Zlomky? Pokraťte je na maximum!

$\left( 2 : \frac{3}{2} \right) : \frac{1}{2} + \left( \frac{5}{6} : \frac{3}{4} \right) : \frac{2}{3}$


$ = \frac{2}{1} \cdot \frac{2}{3} \cdot \frac{2}{1} + \frac{5}{6} \cdot \frac{4}{3} \cdot \frac{3}{2}$

$ = \frac{2}{1} \cdot \frac{2}{3} \cdot \frac{2}{1} + \frac{5}{\cancel{\color{green}6}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{green}{4}}}{3} \cdot \frac{3}{2}$

$ = \frac{2}{1} \cdot \frac{2}{3} \cdot \frac{2}{1} + \frac{5}{3} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{green}{2}}}{\cancel{\color{red}{3}}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{red}{3}}}{\cancel{\color{green}{2}}}$

$ = \frac{2}{1} \cdot \frac{2}{3} \cdot \frac{2}{1} + \frac{5}{3} \cdot \frac{1}{1} \cdot \frac{1}{1}$

$ = \frac{2 \, \cdot \, 2 \, \cdot \, 2}{3 \, \cdot \, 1 \, \cdot \, 1} + \frac{5 \, \cdot \, 1 \, \cdot \, 1}{3 \, \cdot \, 1 \, \cdot \, 1} $

$ = \frac{8}{3} + \frac{5}{3} = \frac{13}{3}$

$$\frac{\frac{13}{10} - 1{,}4}{\frac{2}{15} + \frac{1}{6}}$$


$$\frac{\frac{13}{10} - \frac{14}{10}}{\frac{4 \,+\, 5}{30}} = \frac{\frac{-1}{10}}{\frac{\cancel{\color{red}9}}{\cancel{\color{red}30}}} = \frac{\frac{-1}{10}}{\frac{3}{10}}$$ $= \frac{-1}{\cancel{\color{red}10}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{red}10}}{3} = \frac{-1}{\phantom{-}1} \cdot \frac{\phantom{-}1}{\phantom{-}3} = \frac{-1}{\phantom{-}3}$

2. řádný termín

Krácení zlomků: Váš klíč k matematické eleganci.

$\left(\frac{3}{4} + \frac{4}{3}\right) \cdot \left(\frac{2}{3} - \frac{6}{5}\right)$


$\frac{3 \, \cdot \, 3 \, + \, 4 \, \cdot \, 4}{12} \cdot \frac{2 \, \cdot \, 5 \, - \, 6 \, \cdot \, 3}{15} = \frac{\cancel{\color{red}25}}{\cancel{\color{green}12}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{green}{-8}}}{\cancel{\color{red}15}} = \frac{5}{3} \cdot \frac{-2}{3} = \frac{5\,\cdot\,(-2)}{3\,\cdot\,3} = - \frac{10}{9}$

$$\frac{\frac{5}{9} - \frac{3}{2} : \frac{3}{5}}{\frac{2}{3} + \frac{1}{6} - \frac{7}{12}}$$


$$\frac{\frac{5}{9} - \frac{3}{2} \cdot \frac{5}{3}}{\frac{2}{3} + \frac{1}{6} - \frac{7}{12}} = \frac{ \frac{5}{9} - \frac{\cancel{\color{red} 3}}{2} \cdot \frac{5}{\cancel{\color{red} 3}} }{ \frac{2\,\cdot\,4\,+\,1\,\cdot\,2\,-\,7}{12}}=$$

$$ = \frac{\frac{5}{9} - \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{5}{1}}{\frac{8\,+\,2\,-\,7}{12}} = \frac{\frac{5}{9} - \frac{5}{2}}{\frac{3}{12}} = \frac{\frac{5 \,\cdot\, 2 \,-\, 5 \,\cdot\, 9}{18}}{\frac{\cancel{\color{red} 3}}{\cancel{\color{red} 12}}} $$

$$ = \frac{\frac{10\,-\,45}{18}}{\frac{1}{4}} = \frac{\frac{-35}{18}}{\frac{1}{4}}$$ $= \frac{-35}{\cancel{\color{red}{18}}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{red}{4}}}{1} = \frac{-35}{9} \cdot \frac{2}{1} = \frac{-70}{9}$

1. náhradní termín

Zkrať, co můžeš — ulehčíš si cestu ke správnému výsledku!

$$\frac{\frac{7}{5} + 3{,}3 - \frac{1}{2}}{\frac{1}{15} + \frac{1}{3}}$$


$$ \frac{\frac{7}{5} + \frac{33}{10} - \frac{1}{2}}{\frac{1\,+\,1\,\cdot\,5}{15}} = \frac{\frac{7\,\cdot\,2\,+\,33\,-\,1\,\cdot\,5}{10}}{\frac{1\,+\,5}{15}} = \frac{\frac{4\,+\,33\,+\,5}{10}}{\frac{6}{15}} = \frac{\frac{42}{10}}{\frac{\cancel{\color{red}6}}{\cancel{\color{red}15}}} = \frac{\frac{\cancel{\color{red}42}}{\cancel{\color{red}10}}}{\frac{2}{5}}$$ $ \frac{21}{\cancel{\color{red}5}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{red}5}}{2} = \frac{21}{1} \cdot \frac{1}{2} = \frac{21}{2}$

$\left( \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{3} : \frac{5}{6} \right) - \frac{7}{2} + \frac{3}{5} : \frac{3}{2} - 1$


Nejdříve vyřeším dělení

$\left( \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{\cancel{\color{red}3}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{red}6}}{5} \right) - \frac{7}{2} + \frac{\cancel{\color{red}3}}{5} \cdot \frac{2}{\cancel{\color{red}3}} - 1$

$= \left( \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{1} \cdot \frac{2}{5} \right) - \frac{7}{2} + \frac{1}{5} \cdot \frac{2}{1} - 1$

$= \left( \frac{1}{2} + \frac{2}{5} \right) - \frac{7}{2} + \frac{2}{5} - 1$

$\frac{5\,+\,2\,\cdot\,2}{10} - \frac{7}{2} + \frac{2}{5} - 1$ $\frac{5\,+\,4}{10} - \frac{7}{2} + \frac{2}{5} - 1$ $\frac{9}{10} - \frac{7}{2} + \frac{2}{5} - 1$ $\frac{9\,-\,7\,\cdot\,5\,+\,2\,\cdot\,2\,-\,1\,\cdot\,10}{10}$ $\frac{9\,-\,35\,+\,4\,-\,10}{10}$ $\frac{-32}{10} = -\frac{16}{5}$

$= \frac{9\,-\,35\,+\,4\,-\,10}{10}$ $= \frac{-32}{10} = -\frac{16}{5}$
