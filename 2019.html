1. řádný termín

$(6 - 4) \cdot \frac{11}{8} + \frac{9}{14} \cdot \frac{7}{6}$


$ =(2) \cdot \frac{11}{8} + \frac{9}{14} \cdot \frac{7}{6} =\frac{\cancel{\color{red}2}}{1} \cdot \frac{11}{\cancel{\color{red}8}} + \frac{\cancel{\color{blue}9}}{\cancel{\color{brown}14}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{brown}7}}{\cancel{\color{blue}6}} $ $ =\frac{1}{1} \cdot \frac{11}{4} + \frac{3}{2} \cdot \frac{1}{2} =\frac{11}{4} + \frac{3}{4} =\frac{14}{4} =\frac{\cancel{\color{green}14}}{\cancel{\color{green}4}} =\frac{7}{2} $

$$\frac{\frac{2\,\cdot\,3}{6} - \frac{4}{2\,\cdot\,3}}{\frac{2\,+\,3}{6}}$$


$$ =\frac{\frac{6}{6} - \frac{4}{6}}{\frac{5}{6}} =\frac{\frac{2}{6}}{\frac{5}{6}} $$ $ =\frac{2}{6} \cdot \frac{6}{5} =\frac{2}{\cancel{\color{red}6}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{red}6}}{5} =\frac{2}{1} \cdot \frac{1}{5} =\frac{2}{5} $

2. řádný termín

$$\frac{1 - \frac{1}{3}}{-6^2}$$


$$ =\frac{\frac{3\,-\,1}{3}}{-36} =\frac{\frac{-2}{3}}{\frac{-36}{1}} $$ $ =\frac{2}{3} \cdot \frac{1}{-36} =\frac{\cancel{\color{red}2}}{3} \cdot \frac{1}{\cancel{\color{red}-36}} =\frac{1}{3} \cdot \frac{1}{-18} =-\frac{1}{54} $

$12 \cdot \left(\frac{2}{3} - \frac{1}{2}\right) - \frac{5}{2} + \frac{2}{3}$


$ =12 \cdot \left(\frac{2\,\cdot\,2\,-\,1\,\cdot\,3}{6}\right) - \frac{5}{2} + \frac{2}{3} =\frac{\cancel{\color{red}12}}{1} \cdot \frac{4\,-\,3}{\cancel{\color{red}6}} - \frac{5}{2} + \frac{2}{3} $ $ =\frac{2}{1} \cdot \frac{1}{1} - \frac{5}{2} + \frac{2}{3} =\frac{2}{1} - \frac{5}{2} + \frac{2}{3} =\frac{2\,\cdot\,6\,-\,5\,\cdot\,3\,+\,2\,\cdot\,2}{6} $ $ =\frac{12\,-\,15\,+\,4}{6} =\frac{1}{6} $
