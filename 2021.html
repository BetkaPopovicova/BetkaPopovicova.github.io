1. řádný termín

$\left(\frac{5}{8} \cdot \frac{10}{9} - \frac{4}{9}\right) : \left(8 \cdot \frac{1}{6}\right)$


$ =\left(\frac{5}{\cancel{\color{red}8}} \cdot \frac{\cancel{\color{red}10}}{9} - \frac{4}{9}\right) : \left(\frac{8}{1} \cdot \frac{1}{6}\right) =\left(\frac{5}{4} \cdot \frac{5}{9} - \frac{4}{9}\right) : \left(\frac{\cancel{\color{blue}8}}{1} \cdot \frac{1}{\cancel{\color{blue}6}}\right) $ $ =\left(\frac{25}{36} - \frac{4}{9}\right) : \left(\frac{4}{1} \cdot \frac{1}{3} \right) =\frac{25\,-\,4\,\cdot\,4}{36} : \frac{4}{3} =\frac{25\,-\,16}{36} \cdot \frac{3}{4} $ $ =\frac{\cancel{\color{green}9}}{\cancel{\color{green}36}} \cdot \frac{3}{4} =\frac{1}{4} \cdot \frac{3}{4} =\frac{3}{16} $
